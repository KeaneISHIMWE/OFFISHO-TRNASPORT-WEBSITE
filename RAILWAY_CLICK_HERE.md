## Step 4: Get Your Credentials

Once the database is created:

1. Click on the **MySQL** service (it will appear in your project)
2. Click on the **"Variables"** tab at the top
3. You'll see these values:
   - `MYSQLHOST`
   - `MYSQLUSER`
   - `MYSQLPASSWORD`
   - `MYSQLDATABASE`
   - `MYSQLPORT`

**Copy these values** - you'll need them for Vercel!
